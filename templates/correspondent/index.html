Compliance and Security

NVC Fund Bank maintains rigorous compliance standards for all correspondent banking relationships:

Eligibility Requirements
  • Licensed and regulated financial institution
  • Robust AML/CFT compliance program
  • Sound financial standing and capital adequacy
  • Transparency in ownership and operations
  • Satisfactory regulatory track record
Ongoing Monitoring
  • Regular AML/KYC reviews and assessments
  • Transaction monitoring and suspicious activity reporting
  • Sanctions screening and compliance checks
  • Annual relationship review process
  • Secure communication and data protection protocols
Note: All correspondent banking relationships with NVC Fund Bank are subject to our standard due diligence process and approval by our compliance department.
