Partnership Benefits

Our correspondent banking partners benefit from:

  • Access to NVC Fund Bank's global settlement infrastructure
  • Integration with our proprietary NVCT stablecoin ecosystem
  • Competitive currency exchange rates
  • Streamlined cross-border payment processing
  • Enhanced liquidity management options
  • Comprehensive trade finance facilities
  • Secure, compliant transaction channels
  • Advanced API connectivity for automated processing

Partnership Criteria

NVC Fund Bank selects correspondent banking partners based on:

  • Proper licensing and regulatory oversight
  • Strong financial standing and stability
  • Robust AML/CFT compliance programs
  • Established track record in the financial sector
  • Transparent ownership and governance structures
  • Alignment with NVC Fund Bank's strategic objectives
  • Commitment to maintaining high operational standards
  • Geographic relevance to our global network
