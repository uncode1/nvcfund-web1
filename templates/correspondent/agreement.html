Agreement Overview

The NVC Fund Bank Correspondent Banking Agreement establishes the formal relationship between NVC Fund Bank (as the correspondent bank) and your financial institution (as the respondent bank). This agreement outlines the rights, responsibilities, and obligations of both parties in the correspondent banking relationship.

As a Supranational Sovereign Financial Institution with a balance sheet of over $10 trillion in assets, NVC Fund Bank provides comprehensive correspondent banking services to qualified financial institutions worldwide. Our agreement is designed to facilitate secure, efficient cross-border transactions while ensuring compliance with international standards and regulations.

Key Agreement Components

1. Scope of Services

  • Correspondent account maintenance in multiple currencies
  • International wire transfers via SWIFT
  • Foreign exchange services
  • Real-time gross settlement (RTGS) capabilities
  • NVCT stablecoin ecosystem integration
  • API infrastructure access
  • Liquidity management services
  • Trade finance facilities

Agreement Execution Process

  1. Review the Agreement: Download and thoroughly review the Correspondent Banking Agreement PDF with your legal and compliance teams.
  2. Due Diligence: Complete NVC Fund Bank's due diligence questionnaire and provide all required documentation.
  3. Negotiate Terms: Discuss any specific requirements or adjustments needed to the standard agreement.
  4. Obtain Internal Approvals: Secure necessary approvals from your institution's management and board as required.
  5. Sign the Agreement: Execute the final agreement with authorized signatories from both institutions.
  6. Account Setup: Complete the account opening process and operational implementation.
