Custody Services Overview

NVC Fund Bank offers institutional-grade custody services for both traditional and digital assets. As a Supranational Sovereign Financial Institution with over $10 trillion in balance sheet assets, we provide highly secure, compliant custody solutions for a wide range of financial institutions.

Step 1: Initial Assessment

Required Information
  • Basic institutional information
  • Asset types requiring custody
  • Estimated asset values
  • Current custody arrangements
  • Primary custody requirements
  • Regulatory framework
  • Security and reporting needs
Timeline: 1-2 business days for initial assessment

Step 2: Custody Solution Design

Process Overview
  • In-depth custody needs analysis
  • Security protocol planning
  • Account structure design
  • Asset transfer planning
  • Reporting customization
  • Fee structure determination
  • Security access protocols
Timeline: 1 week for solution design

Step 3: Agreement & Documentation

Agreement Process
  • Review of custody agreement
  • Service level agreement development
  • Fee schedule confirmation
  • Asset transfer authorization documentation
  • Authorized signatory registration
  • Compliance documentation
  • Agreement signing by authorized parties
Timeline: 1 week for agreement finalization

Step 4: Implementation & Asset Transfer

Implementation Process
  • Custody account setup
  • Security credentials distribution
  • Asset transfer coordination
  • Reporting system implementation
  • Client portal access configuration
  • User training and documentation
  • Ongoing custody management
Timeline: 1-2 weeks for implementation
