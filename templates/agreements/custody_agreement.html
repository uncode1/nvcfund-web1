Agreement Overview

The NVC Fund Bank Custody Services Agreement establishes the formal relationship between NVC Fund Bank (as the custodian) and your institution (as the client). This agreement outlines the rights, responsibilities, and obligations of both parties in the custody relationship.

As a Supranational Sovereign Financial Institution with a balance sheet of over $10 trillion in assets, NVC Fund Bank provides comprehensive custody services for both traditional and digital assets to qualified institutional clients worldwide. Our agreement is designed to establish clear guidelines for the safekeeping and administration of your assets while ensuring compliance with international standards and regulations.
