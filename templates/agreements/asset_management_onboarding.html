Onboarding Process Overview

NVC Fund Bank's asset management services are designed for institutional clients seeking expert management of their investment portfolios. With over $10 trillion in balance sheet assets, our investment team brings exceptional expertise to every portfolio we manage.

Step 1: Initial Consultation

Required Information
  • Basic institutional information
  • Investment objectives
  • Current asset allocation
  • Risk tolerance assessment
  • Investment time horizon
  • Liquidity requirements
  • Regulatory constraints
Timeline: 1-2 business days for initial consultation

Step 2: Investment Strategy Development

Process Overview
  • In-depth review of investment needs
  • Asset allocation modeling
  • Risk analysis and stress testing
  • Investment policy statement development
  • Fee structure determination
  • Performance benchmarking selection
  • Customized strategy presentation
Timeline: 1-2 weeks for strategy development

Step 3: Agreement & Documentation

Agreement Process
  • Review of asset management agreement
  • Investment policy statement finalization
  • Fee schedule confirmation
  • Investment mandate documentation
  • Discretionary authority parameters
  • Compliance requirements review
  • Agreement signing by authorized signatories
Timeline: 1 week for agreement finalization

Step 4: Portfolio Implementation

Implementation Process
  • Asset transfer coordination
  • Account setup and configuration
  • Initial portfolio construction
  • Implementation of investment strategy
  • Reporting system setup
  • Client portal access
  • Initial performance review schedule
Timeline: 1-2 weeks for implementation
