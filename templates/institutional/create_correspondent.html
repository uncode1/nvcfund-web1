About Correspondent Banking

Correspondent banking is an arrangement where one bank provides services on behalf of another. There are two main types of accounts:

  • Nostro Account ("Ours"): An account that your bank holds with a correspondent bank, typically in a foreign country. Example: NVC Bank's USD account at a bank in the United States.
  • Vostro Account ("Yours"): An account that a correspondent bank holds with your bank. Example: A foreign bank's NVCT account at NVC Bank.

These accounts facilitate cross-border payments, forex transactions, and trade finance.
