Understanding transaction settlement processes

Settlement Process Overview

Transaction settlement is the process of finalizing and clearing financial transactions between parties through the NVC Banking Platform.

Settlement Flow

Initiation

Transaction request submitted

Validation

Security and compliance checks

Processing

Fund movement and clearing

Settlement

Final confirmation and record

Settlement Types

  • Real-time Settlement: Immediate processing for internal transfers
  • Batch Settlement: End-of-day processing for external transfers
  • SWIFT Settlement: International wire transfer clearing
  • Blockchain Settlement: Smart contract execution for NVCT transactions

Settlement Timeframes

  • Internal NVC transfers: Immediate
  • Domestic wire transfers: Same business day
  • International SWIFT transfers: 1-3 business days
  • ACH transfers: 1-2 business days
