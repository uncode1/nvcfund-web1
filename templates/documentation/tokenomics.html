NVCT Token Overview

The NVC Token (NVCT) is the native digital currency of the NVC Banking Platform, designed to facilitate seamless transactions and provide utility within the ecosystem.

Token Utility

Economic Model

NVCT maintains its 1:1 USD peg through full reserve backing, ensuring stability and trust. The token supply is dynamically managed based on demand and reserve requirements.
