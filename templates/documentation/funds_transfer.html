NVC Funds Transfer Guide

Complete guide for funds transfer operations

Overview

This guide covers the complete process for transferring funds through the NVC Banking Platform, including supported methods, requirements, and best practices for institutional clients.

Supported Transfer Methods

  • SWIFT Network: International wire transfers using our 15 global SWIFT codes across 4 continents
  • ACH Processing: Automated Clearing House transfers for domestic US transactions
  • Internal Transfers: Instant transfers between NVC Bank accounts worldwide
  • Correspondent Banking: Through our network of 500+ partner institutions
  • Real-time Payments: Same-day settlement for urgent transfers
  • Blockchain Transfers: NVCT token transfers on Ethereum network

Transfer Limits and Requirements

Daily Transfer Limits

  • Individual accounts: $500,000 USD equivalent
  • Business accounts: $5,000,000 USD equivalent
  • Institutional accounts: $100,000,000 USD equivalent
  • Treasury operations: Unlimited (with authorization)

Required Documentation

  • Valid account authentication and dual authorization
  • Sufficient available balance plus transfer fees
  • Enhanced due diligence for amounts over $10,000
  • Complete beneficiary information including SWIFT/IBAN codes
  • Purpose of payment documentation for international transfers
  • Regulatory compliance certificates for cross-border transactions

Processing Times

  • Internal NVC transfers: Immediate
  • Domestic wire transfers: Same business day
  • International SWIFT transfers: 1-3 business days
  • ACH transfers: 1-2 business days
  • Correspondent banking: 2-5 business days
  • NVCT blockchain: 5-15 minutes

Fee Structure

  • Internal transfers: No charge
  • Domestic wire: $25 USD
  • International SWIFT: $45 USD + correspondent fees
  • ACH transfers: $3 USD
  • Express/same-day: Additional $50 USD
